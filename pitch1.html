Problem

What is broken today?

"The issue is not a lack of listings. The issue is a lack of decision support."

  • • Fragmented Inputs: Users piece together commute, safety, and amenities across disconnected tools.
  • • Generic Ranking: Platforms optimize for popular inventory, not the specific user.
  • • Lifestyle Blind Spots: You cannot search by daily routine or transit habits.

Solution

What PulsePath Is

A conversational platform that turns priorities into clear neighborhood recommendations.

Tailored to lifestyle, not just price.
Context-aware and adaptive.
Action-oriented handoffs.
Explainable decision paths.

Growth Potential

Why this can scale

Start with a narrow transition market, then expand into a broader life-stage product.

Focused launch wedge

Incoming students and users moving from dorm-style or temporary housing into off-campus living.

Initial geographies

University-centered ecosystems such as Seattle and the SF Bay Area, where housing costs and transition friction are both high.

Expansion path

Grow from campus-adjacent users into broader early-career and urban renter segments facing the same decision problem.
